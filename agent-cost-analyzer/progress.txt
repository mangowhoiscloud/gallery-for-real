Why: Population stddev for anomaly detection — self-contained formula with no external dependency. Tests require ≥10 normal entries before the outlier to guarantee detection: derived from n>9 inequality from algebraic threshold formula.
Discovered: With population 3σ and small sample sizes (n<10), even a large outlier can fail to be detected because it inflates the mean and stddev enough to keep itself below the threshold.
